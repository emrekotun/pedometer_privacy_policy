# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 17, 2026

## Summary

Pedometer does not collect, store, or share any of your personal data. All health and activity data stays on your device and is never transmitted to any server.

---

## Data Collection

**Pedometer collects no data.** The app has no backend servers, no analytics SDKs, no crash reporting tools, and no advertising frameworks. There is no account system, no sign-up, and no login.

| Data Type | Collected | Purpose |
|-----------|-----------|---------|
| Step count | No | — |
| Distance walked/run | No | — |
| Flights climbed | No | — |
| Workout sessions | No | — |
| Daily goals / settings | No | — |
| Device identifiers | No | — |
| Location | No | — |
| IP address | No | — |
| Crash logs | No | — |
| Usage analytics | No | — |

---

## Health & Activity Data

Pedometer reads step count, walking/running distance, and flights climbed from **Apple HealthKit** and/or **Core Motion** — Apple's built-in on-device frameworks.

- Data is read **directly on your device**.
- Data is displayed in the app and stored **locally in UserDefaults and App Groups** (for widget sharing).
- Data is **never transmitted** off your device.
- You can revoke Pedometer's access at any time via **Settings → Privacy → Health** on iOS.

---

## Notifications

If you enable daily reminders, Pedometer uses local notifications scheduled on your device. No notification data is sent to any server.

---

## Data Export

Pedometer offers a CSV export feature. The exported file contains your step history and is generated **entirely on your device**. When you share this file (e.g., via Share Sheet), it is controlled entirely by you and your chosen share destination.

---

## Third-Party Services

Pedometer does **not** integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or external SDKs. The app relies solely on Apple's first-party frameworks:

- **HealthKit** — read step count, distance, flights climbed
- **Core Motion** — read step count (watchOS)
- **UserNotifications** — schedule daily reminders
- **UserDefaults / App Groups** — local persistence and widget sharing

---

## Apple Watch & Widgets

The Apple Watch companion app reads step data via Core Motion directly on your watch. The iOS widget reads your latest step count from a shared App Group container. All data remains on-device.

---

## Children's Privacy

Pedometer does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has shared data through this app, contact us — though no data is collected in the first place.

---

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date at the top will be revised. Since Pedometer does not collect data, changes will generally reflect App Store requirements or clarify existing practices.
